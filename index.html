United States Population Health and Lifestyle Dashboard


Dashboard Summary

The dashboard below provides users with upto date information regarding what lifestyle factors can effect the health of people in various states within the US. Datasets pertaining to health, lifestyle, state GDP, smoking, obesity, access to healthcare, environmental factors, age and poverty rates have been consolidated into the dashboard for users select these parameters to gain a visual if there are links between, for example, access to healthcare and poverty rates or the proporption of proportion of prison populations to the overall population of the state. Click on the labels within the x and y axis to gain a visual of the dataset.
